ABOUT US

BRAND CONCEPT

“Glassic Eyewear” is a independant eyewear brand located in Taiwan. The essence of the brand is embodied by creating a distinctive eyewear.

With modern craft, contemporary inspiration and ergonomic design, “Glassic” convices by a subtle and sophisticated style, designed to last over fades and trends.

CRAFTSMANSHIP

Our products are produced in Taiwan, boasting the top-level quality in the world.

The metal, acetate, lens and minute parts are carefully supported one by one by the techniques of professional craftsmen who work hard and learn from each other. They are simply beyond the area of products, luring you into the world of art.

FOUNFER /
DESIGNER

For over 10 years, based on OEM business, Brenda Lawrence has been offering designs and projects to various fashion eyewear brands in Taiwan and overseas.

Her design technique is unique and extremely delicate. She begins with handwriting, makes it into data and then cut and mold with her own hands.

In the fall of 2021, she forms the long-awaited original brand of her own, “Glassic Eyewear”.

Jones M. is a collection of rounded and slightly drop-shaped frames. The shape of the lens is inspired by vintage aviator glasses, but framed in a new way.

Taylor C. is a collection of hexagonal-shaped frames. The shape of the lens is inspired by vintage glasses, but renovated in a new and trendy way.

Davies E.

Davies E. is a collection of square-shaped eyeglasses. This timeless unisex shape is all about the details. The inner upper edge is carefully slanted to keep things interesting, while staying true to the classic.

A good fit for today's standards and for years to come.
